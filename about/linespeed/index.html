LineSpeed

Based in Perth Northern Suburbs, WA, LINESPEED Athletic Performance (LAPT) was formed to improve running speed of athletes from various sporting codes.

Mission

Develop a CULTURE that encourages athletes to strive for EXCELLENCE in sport, and in life.

Values

  • Determination

    Believing in yourself to continue to do the best you can even if things are difficult. Making or arriving at a decision with purpose.

  • Respect

    Being fair, knowing your limits whilst taking care of yourself and others around you. Respect denotes both a positive feeling of esteem for another person and also specific actions and conduct representative of that esteem.

  • Courage

    Being brave and confident enough to do what you believe is right. Courage is the ability to confront fear, pain, risk/danger, uncertainty or intimidation. “Moral courage” is the ability to act rightly in the face of popular opposition, shame, scandal, or discouragement.

  • Friendship

    Understanding each other although there may be differences. Values that are found in friendships are often a result of a friend demonstrating the following on a consistent basis: sympathy, empath, honesty, mutual understanding, compassion, trust.

LAPT Affirmations

  • Gratitude

    Appreciating and being thankful for NOW. Being grateful for the opportunity to train, to be able to improve your skill, for being amongst like minded athletes who strive to be better.

  • Self-Belief

    To believe in nothing else, except that you will get BETTER. With every training you partake, focus on improving one thing at a time. No matter how slow or small you improve, progress is progress. Which leads to success.

  • Inspire

    Find your why. Someone that inspires you to be better, to be great.
    Then in chasing that greatness, while your striving towards your goals, someone is watching you, at games, at training, then in turn, you INSPIRE them.
    You have power to EMPOWER others through your actions.

  • Excellence

    Accept nothing but your BEST. In everything you do, warm up, Cool down, prepping for training the day before, making your bed. It all matters. Put your best to everything you do, and you will be successful, win or LEARN, the journey
