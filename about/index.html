Family - Faith - Culture - Community

I am the father of 5 highly active children, Keenan (19yrs), Lennox (12yrs), Peytton (10yrs), Bryson (9yrs) and Zoe (7yrs) and husband to a trophy wife Finah

I have been passionately coaching youth in various sports codes (Rugby League, Basketball, Athletics) in Perth WA for over 10 years and still am actively sports coaching Rugby League with Alkimos Tigers and Athletics with Butler Boomers.

Born and raised in South AucklandnNZ amongst my Samoan Catholic family, I was heavily involved in various sports codes throughout my up bringing. Combined with a strong leadership employment history, I came to Perth WA in 2007 and was able to evolve into a youth coaching machine founded on a values-based approach and building a community culture of hard-working athletes.

Through observing common differences in youth sports-performance across codes, I recognised speed and agility as the most impactful factor affecting success in youth.

Commenced a journey with my fab 5 in 2018 where I stepped down from a management role and committed myself to spending more time with family and coaching my kids in strength, power and SPEED.

As we progressed, and successful outcomes directly related to our training were evident, I opened up our sessions to our community where Linespeed was created in 2019.
